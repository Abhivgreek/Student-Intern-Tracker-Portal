About Student Internship Tracker

We're on a mission to help students manage their internship journey more effectively, from application to completion.

Our platform was created by students who experienced the challenges of managing multiple internship applications and opportunities. We understand the importance of internships in building a successful career and want to make the process as smooth as possible.

Our Mission

To empower students to take control of their professional development through effective internship management.

For Students

Helping students organize and track their internship journey

For Institutions

Providing tools for educational institutions to monitor student progress

For Companies

Creating a bridge between students and internship providers

Our Story

The Student Internship Tracker was born out of a real need. As students ourselves, we struggled to keep track of multiple internship applications, deadlines, and progress.

What started as a simple spreadsheet to track our own internships quickly evolved into a comprehensive platform that could help students everywhere.

Today, our platform is used by thousands of students across the country to manage their internship journey, from application to completion.

2025

Launch year: Portal launched with core features — registration, login, dashboard, and basic internship tracking.

2026

Working on tracking, role-based access for coordinators, and integrated email notifications. Will be Partnered with 50+ universities.

2027

AI-Powered Matching & Analytics: smart internship recommendations and dashboards with analytics for students and colleges will be available
